📖 How to Play Echo Chamber

🎯 Your Mission

You are the unseen hand behind a massive social media feed.

You don't write the posts — you decide what millions of people see.

Every choice shapes society's mood and the platform's engagement.

🌐

Your algorithm decides reality

📱 How It Works

1 Review Posts: Each day, you'll see 3 posts with different tones and topics.
2 Make Decisions: For each post, choose one action:
  • Boost Amplify the post's reach to millions
  • Hide Suppress the post from the feed
  • Ignore Let it pass naturally
3 Watch Society React: Your decisions affect:
  • 📈 Engagement: How active users are
  • 😊 Mood: Society's emotional state
  • 🏙️ The City: Watch stick figures react!

⚖️ The Dilemma

There is no "correct" way to play.

Every strategy has consequences:

🔥 Boost negative content:
High engagement but society becomes angry
❤️ Boost positive content:
Happy society but lower engagement
🚫 Hide too much:
People lose trust and leave the platform

🎭 Watch the City

The stick figures reveal society's state:

😊 Happy (Mood > 20):
Bouncing, waving, peaceful
😐 Normal (-10 to 20):
Walking calmly
😠 Angry (-30 to -10):
Fast walking, agitated
🔥 Chaos (< -30):
Running, fighting, riots!

🌈 Harmony
